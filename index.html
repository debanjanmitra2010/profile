✹ Analysis of a Triple Band Notched CPW-fed Planar UWB Antenna (2018)- A microstrip-fed triple band notch UWB monopole antenna is designed. The antenna uses different notching techniques to implement three band notches at 5.5 GHz, 6.6 GHz and 8.1 GHz respectively. A semi-circular slot on the radiator is used for implementing the 5.5 GHz notch. The 6.6 GHz notch is implemented using a pair of square C-shaped slots in the ground plane. And the 8.1 GHz notch is implemented using parasitic strips. The designed antenna has the dimensions of 25 mm x 29 mm. The designed antenna has stable radiation patterns and has a peak gain of 5 dBi. The proposed antenna is distortion-less as the group delay profile is within 1ns.
